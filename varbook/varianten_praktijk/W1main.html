House at Kasama
Kasama, Ibaraki, 1980-1981, Toyo ItoGA Houses
A.D.A. EDITA Tokyo

 



This house is situated on a steeply sloped wooded site 100 kilometres north of Tokyo. The construction consists of a combination of wood and concrete. The plan is T-shaped in order to avail the house to sunlight and vistas and to organise all the required functions such as a workshop and a gallery. Natural light enters the rooms from different directions and splits the interior making the character of space ambiguous. An example can be seen on the photograph on the right.
